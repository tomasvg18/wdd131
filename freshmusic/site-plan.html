Overview

Purpose

Fresh Music is a copyright free song library organized by genre. Users can explore and enjoy music!

Audience

Fresh Music is designed for students, creators, and casual listeners looking for copyright-free music to relax, focus, or use in projects.

Dynamic elements

A drop down menu or button menu to select a genre of music, JS to filter the array of songs and display them, Playable audio with controls like forward, back, pause, play, Random song feature, show currently playing song in a display box.
